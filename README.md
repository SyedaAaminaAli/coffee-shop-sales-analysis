# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to understand customer behavior, identify revenue trends, and provide recommendations to improve business performance. The analysis follows the Google Data Analytics process: Ask, Prepare, Process, Analyze, Share, and Act.

## Business Task

The coffee shop owner wants to understand sales patterns and identify opportunities to increase revenue.

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics

## Key Findings

### Product Category Analysis
- Coffee generated the highest revenue ($269,952).
- Tea was the second highest category ($196,406).

### Peak Hours
- Sales peaked during the morning hours.
- 10 AM was the busiest hour.

### Weekday Analysis
- Monday generated the highest revenue ($101,677).
- Saturday recorded the lowest revenue ($96,894).

### Store Location Analysis
- Hell's Kitchen generated the highest revenue ($236,511).
- Astoria and Lower Manhattan followed closely.

## Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing coffee and tea products.
- Ensure adequate staffing during peak hours.
- Introduce promotions during weekends.
- Apply successful practices from Hell's Kitchen to other locations.
