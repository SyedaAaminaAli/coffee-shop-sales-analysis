# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using Microsoft Excel and Pivot Tables.  
The goal is to identify sales trends, customer behavior, top-performing products, and store performance to support data-driven business decisions.

The analysis follows the **Google Data Analytics Process**:
Ask → Prepare → Process → Analyze → Share → Act

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
The main objectives of this analysis are:
- Identify top-selling products and categories
- Analyze sales trends by time, day, and month
- Evaluate store performance
- Discover peak sales hours
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🥇 Product Performance
- Coffee is the highest revenue-generating category
- Tea is the second most profitable category
- Bakery and drinks contribute moderately to revenue

### ⏰ Peak Hours Analysis
- Highest customer activity occurs between 8 AM – 10 AM
- Morning hours generate the most revenue

### 📅 Weekly Trends
- Sales are consistent throughout the week
- Slight drop observed on weekends

### 🏬 Store Performance
- Hell’s Kitchen has the highest revenue
- Astoria and Lower Manhattan show similar performance
